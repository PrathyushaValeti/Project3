TATA NEXON

The Tata Nexon has 1 Diesel Engine and 1 Petrol Engine on offer. The Diesel engine is 1497 cc while the Petrol engine is 1199 cc. It is available with Automatic & Manual transmission.Depending upon the variant and fuel type the Nexon has a mileage of 17.01 to 24.08 kmpl & Ground clearance of Nexon is 208 mm. Thus was born the Tata Nexon, the popular crossover SUV, that has since built up a steely reputation for its safety features, affordability, road presence, and performance. When introduced, the Nexon became the country's first made-in-India, sold-in-India model to register a five-star rating from Global NCAP High safety standards, modern features and futuristic design back its success. Almost all Tata cars are doing well for the brand, but the Nexon is doing exceptionally well for the brand.
